Target Audience

Targeted audience for the Elk Point Chamber of Commerce is to support and assist businesses within the municipality, help them network together, and improve upon community business environments. Businesses that have a sense of community and support, work together to sustain, develop, and help each other and the community in which they are based.

Persona One

New member of the community. Uses their mobile browser to research and locate local businesses and make connections. Interested in possibly opening a business in town.

Persona Two

Business Owner. Uses their laptop during business hours to join the chamber, locate information in regard to other businesses, and community events.

Scenarios

How do I become a member of the chamber of Commerce? What are the fees?

Who are the board members for the chamber?

How do I reach members of the chamber?

Where do I find information and hours of operations for local businesses.

Are there events, trade shows, or community activities taking place?
