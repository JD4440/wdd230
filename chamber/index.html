Teddy for a Toonie

ATB Financial teddy for a toonie fundraiser had another successful year thanks to the community and its support. Over $3,000 was raised in Elk Point between teddy bear sales, bake sale, BBQ, the teddy and quilt raffles. ATB will be matching the raised amount of each of the branches and donating the funds to the childrens hospital.
