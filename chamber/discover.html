Haying In The 30s

A yearly event hosted to fundraise for cancer patients. Everything is done as it would have been in the 30s, even the clothing of those volunteers are from that era. Interactive and informative for all ages who attend. Take home a piece of your own spun rope, enjoy a wagon ride, and learning about how the agriculture industry worked in the past.
